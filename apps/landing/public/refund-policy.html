Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard settings. Upon cancellation, your access to premium features will continue until the end of the current billing cycle. We do not charge cancellation fees.

2. Refund Policy

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not completely satisfied with our services, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • After the 7-day window, no refunds will be issued for the current billing cycle.
  • Enterprise and custom plans are non-refundable unless specified in a separate agreement.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order ID and the reason for the request.
